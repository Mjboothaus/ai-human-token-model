Excited to share a new repo: **AI-Human Token Dynamics**  
An interactive marimo model for understanding what happens when AI output scales faster than human review capacity.

It frames AI-assisted work as a consumer-resource system:
- AI generates tokens quickly
- Humans process and validate them under real attention limits
- Backlog, throughput, and saturation dynamics emerge naturally

What’s included:
- Interactive notebook (`notebooks/main.py`) with tunable model parameters
- Multiple simulation modes (continuous ODE + discrete variants)
- Visualisations for backlog evolution, equilibrium, and team-size scaling
- References spanning ecological dynamics, human-AI collaboration, and people-risk governance

Why this matters:
Many teams optimise for output volume, but practical value depends on sustainable human-AI throughput, quality control, and safety.

If you’re working on AI operations, workflow design, or governance, I’d love your feedback.
